    Government Solar Energy Schemes

    Explore major Government of India schemes that support solar panel installation and renewable energy adoption.

    Pradhan Mantri Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana

    This scheme promotes rooftop solar installation for households by providing subsidies and up to 300 units of free electricity per month.

    Official Website

    National Portal for Rooftop Solar

    A centralized online portal for applying, tracking, and receiving subsidies for rooftop solar installations across India.

    Official Website

    Grid-Connected Rooftop Solar Programme (MNRE)

    A government programme that provides financial assistance for grid-connected rooftop solar systems to increase clean energy usage.

    Official Website

    PM-KUSUM Scheme

    This scheme supports farmers by promoting solar water pumps and decentralized solar power plants to reduce diesel usage.
