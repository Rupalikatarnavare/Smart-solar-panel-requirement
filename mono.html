What is a Monocrystalline Solar Panel?

Monocrystalline solar panels are manufactured from a single, high-purity silicon crystal and deliver the highest efficiency.A Monocrystalline Solar Panel is made from a single, pure silicon crystal, which allows electrons to move more freely. Because of this, it offers the highest efficiency and best performance among all solar panel types.

  • 17% – 24% efficiency
  • Space efficient
  • 25–40 year lifespan
